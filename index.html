01 // MANIFESTO

I walked away from the machine that was optimizing click-through rates.

The most powerful optimization algorithms on Earth are deployed to keep you scrolling, buying, and distracted. Meanwhile, municipal shelters operate on spreadsheets from 2003. Food banks guess at demand. Emergency services react to crises that were predictable weeks in advance.

The Civic Bridge Initiative exists because the infrastructure to fix this already exists—it just hasn't been pointed at the right target. We're building predictive AI systems that optimize resource allocation for vulnerable populations, deploying on salvaged consumer hardware, and open-sourcing everything.

This isn't research. This isn't a thesis. This is operational architecture running in the real world, right now, with the Salvation Army.

No corporate sponsors. No VC mandates. Just civic math.
